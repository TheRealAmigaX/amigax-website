Crystal (クリスタル)

“She is an autistic tomboy who is friendly and a sweetheart (despite being really shy) who constantly gives advice to others and herself. She likes her hobbies (such as sketching, playing video games, creating music, exploring, and so on). Roboto is her robot companion pet, and he frequently brightens her day.”

Information:

  • Birthdate: 1/15/2006 ( Month / Day / Year )
  • Age: 17
  • Height: 4’6 ft
  • Weight: 89 lb
  • Nationality: Japanese
  • Personality:
    • Normal Form: Sweetheart, Shy, Friendly
    • Glowing/Dark Form: Gloomy, Sad, Lonely
  • Date of Creation: January 15th, 2022 (Original Design), March 15th, 2023 (New Design)

Trivia:

  • She gets depressed often since she often feels lonely. This results in her Glowing/Dark form, where she is much more gloomy. It also causes a change in her appearance, with glowing eyes, lips, and checks, and six back crystals instead of two (Glowing Form Only).
  • When she is embarrassed, she will cover her face with her ears.
  • She is a pacifist who adores nature and abhors both pollution and destruction.
  • She is gem collector, often finding them while wandering around.
  • She is Rainboo's cousin (despite Rainboo being from another planet and Crystal being from Japan).
