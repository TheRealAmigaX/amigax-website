Roboto

“Roboto is a heavy but capable dude built from old computer parts and hardware, capable of outrunning a bullet train, preparing food in less than a minute, and doubling as a computer, among other things. He is, of course, primarily Crystal's robotic companion or AmigaX's Channel Mascot.”

Information:

  • Build date: 4/5/2006 ( Month / Day / Year )
  • Age: 17
  • Height: 2'5 ft
  • Weight: 95 lb
  • Nationality: American/Japanese
  • Personality: Cheery, Happy, Calm
  • Date of Creation: 2015 (Original Design), September 2021 (New Design)

Trivia:

  • He is mainly built from old American computer parts (Commodore 64, Amiga, Atari ST, IBM Computers, Macintosh, etc), while his screen is custom made from an old discarded CRT television screen and his body is made from old industrial machine parts.
  • His top speed is 250 mph (400 km/h), although he usually runs at 70 mph cause his battery runs out easily when running at such high speeds and the risk of the battery potentially exploding.
  • It takes him upwards to 5 hours to recharged, his overall battery life is 60 hours while in idle mode and up to 12 hours when he is running at 70 mph.
  • When Roboto gets corrupted, he will go insane and start malfunctioning, doing all weird things like saying random stuff (usually gibberish), his screen glitching (which gets worse the longer he is corrupted) and often rebooting for no reason, it is recommended he is in a cold climate as his battery will not overheat.
  • He was first created as my OC in 2015-2016 in Blocksworld, his modern version was first created in September of 2021.
