Crystal (クリスタル)

"Despite often being really shy and having trust issues, she is friendly and won't be harsh to you, just don't try to hug her though (unless your Roboto) since she doesnt like it."

Information:

  • Birthdate: 1/15/2006 ( Month / Day / Year )
  • Age: 18
  • Height: 4’6 ft
  • Weight: 89 lb
  • Nationality: Japanese
  • Personality:
    • Normal Form: Shy, Friendly, Sweetheart
    • Dark Form: Gloomy, Sad, Lonely
  • Date of Creation: January 15th, 2022 (Original Design), January 25th, 2024 (New Design)

Trivia:

  • She gets depressed sometimes since she often feels lonely. This results in her Dark form, where she is much more gloomy. It also causes a change in her appearance, with glowing eyes, lips, and checks, and six back crystals instead of two (Glowing Form Only).
  • When she is embarrassed, she will cover her face with her ears.
  • She is a pacifist who adores nature and abhors both pollution and destruction.
  • She is gem collector, often finding them while wandering around.
  • She is Rainboo's cousin (despite Rainboo being from another planet and Crystal being from Japan).
