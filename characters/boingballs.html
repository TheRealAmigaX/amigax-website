The Boing Balls

"A bunch of six boing balls, each with their own personality. Amiga (Red, Male) is the leader and is always thrilled, VIC (Orange, Male) is the shy one who is always playing it safe, Commodor (Yellow, Female) is the cheerful one who is always joyous, and C128 (Green, Male) is the curious one who is constantly wondering about everything. Petty (Blue, Male) is the fearful one who constantly gets into trouble, while C-Plus (Purple, Female) is the gentle one who always brightens up the others."
